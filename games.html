The Game Library
Play That Contributes to Understanding

MR.Play turns traditional research activities into interactive game experiences.

As you make choices, organize ideas, compare options, or respond to challenges, your participation can help researchers better understand preferences and decision-making — while feeling more active, intuitive, and engaging than a conventional survey.
